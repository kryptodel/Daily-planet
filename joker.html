The Joker Strikes Again — Chaos Erupts in Downtown Metropolis

By Lois Lane
July 19, 2026

The Joker laughing maniacally during his latest attack in Metropolis

Chaos returned to Metropolis today as the Clown Prince of Crime, The Joker, made a shocking appearance in the heart of the city. Witnesses report that the villain unleashed a wave of laughing gas and explosive pranks, causing panic and multiple injuries.

Authorities are still trying to determine how The Joker managed to bypass security and enter Metropolis undetected. This marks his third major attack in the region this year, showing a disturbing escalation in his campaign of terror.

Why So Serious?

As always, The Joker left behind his signature calling card — a purple playing card with a sinister grin. Attached was a note that simply read: "Let's put a smile on that face, Metropolis!"

"All it takes is one bad day to turn the sanest man alive into a raving lunatic. That's how far I'm willing to go to prove my point."

Batman and the Justice League have been alerted, but as of now, The Joker remains at large. Citizens are urged to remain vigilant and report any suspicious activity immediately.

This latest attack serves as a grim reminder that even in a city protected by heroes, the shadow of chaos is never far away.
