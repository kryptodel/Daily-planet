Krypto Saves the Day Once Again — The Super Dog Who Needs No Cape

By Lois Lane

Daily Planet Senior Reporter

Krypto soaring above Metropolis after neutralizing LexCorp's drone fleet

For the third time this month, Metropolis was saved not by its famous Man of Steel, but by a white-furred, super-powered dog named Krypto.

Yesterday afternoon, LexCorp unleashed a massive fleet of armed drones over the city. While panic spread through downtown, Krypto appeared in a blur of white and red, destroying the entire squadron in under 60 seconds.

Loyalty Above All

Witnesses described the scene as both terrifying and heartwarming. The super dog, known for his unwavering loyalty to Superman and the people of Metropolis, didn't hesitate for a second. He used his heat vision, super strength, and incredible speed to neutralize every threat without causing a single civilian injury.

"Krypto doesn't need money, fame, or recognition. He just shows up, does what's right, and flies away with his tail wagging. If only all of us could be a little more like him."

Sources close to Superman say that Krypto has been particularly active lately, almost as if he senses the growing threats from LexCorp. The billionaire industrialist has once again denied any involvement, calling the incident "another example of alien interference."

The People's Hero

Social media has exploded with praise for the super dog. Hashtags like #GoodBoyKrypto and #KryptoSavesTheDay are trending worldwide. Children across Metropolis are wearing homemade red capes in his honor, and several local businesses have announced "Krypto Treat Day" promotions.

While Superman remains the symbol of hope for many, Krypto has quietly become the heart of the city — a reminder that sometimes the greatest heroes have four legs and a wagging tail.

As one citizen put it while watching Krypto fly away: "He might be a super dog, but to us, he's just... ours."
